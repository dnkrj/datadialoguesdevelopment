Data dialogues

Nesta and the Scottish Government have recently concluded an 18-month dialogue with Scottish citizens.

We wanted to better understand their opinions and ideas about the sharing of health and care data.

This website presents our findings.

After hearing many different perspectives, we identified 7 distinct relationships that Scottish people have with data.

This website presents these relationships through a series of conversations with future Scottish citizens.

Each fictional dialogue illustrates people’s hopes and fears for the future.
